Serikat Newbie Indonesia (SNI) is a passionate community of cybersecurity enthusiasts based in Indonesia. Our mission is to foster a supportive environment for individuals interested in cybersecurity, whether they are beginners or experienced professionals. Through collaboration, learning, and hands-on experience, we aim to enhance our skills and knowledge in various areas of cybersecurity, including but not limited to penetration testing, digital forensics, cryptography, and web exploitation.
